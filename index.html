Our Mission
No innocent person should lose their freedom because they aren’t given proper access to DNA testing. In order to give DNA the attention it deserves, we’re putting it in front of lawmakers, literally. We’re creating ink that contains the DNA of prisoners who could be proven innocent, and using that ink to write letters to lawmakers, pleading for change. DNA is irrefutable. The quality of our laws and policies should match the quality of our science so that innocent people don’t have to suffer.
How It Works
Every drop of our ink contains the DNA of prisoners that could be proven innocent, if tested.
Step 1
DNA Collection
We research and collect the DNA from wrongfully convicted prisoners.
Step 2
Turn into ink
A lab takes the prisoners DNA and uses it to create our ink.
Step 3
Send letters
We use the ink to spread our message to lawmakers, encouraging change.
